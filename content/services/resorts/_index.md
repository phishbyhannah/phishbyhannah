I provide creative marketing and content packages for tourism operators and resorts — designed to capture the guest experience and turn it into visual storytelling that drives bookings.

## Packages

### The Shoreline (Essentials)

Half-day content shoot *(3–4 hours)*.

Includes:
- In-water + on-land photography
- Up to 40 professionally edited images
- Drone footage *(if permitted)*

### The Tidal Flow (Photo + Video)

Full-day content shoot *(6–8 hours)*.

Includes:
- In-water + on-land + behind-the-scenes storytelling
- Up to 80 edited images
- 3 short-form video clips
- Drone footage *(if permitted)*

### The Drift (Multi-day Photo + Video)

Multi-day tour coverage *(2–5 days)*.

Includes:
- Full content coverage of the guest experience
- Daily delivery of edited images for marketing use
- Video reels + storytelling captions

Notes:
- Travel + accommodation are typically covered for remote locations.
